# BCG Firm Context

## About BCG
Boston Consulting Group (BCG) is a global management consulting firm. Engagements are staffed with lean teams of 2-6 consultants:
- 1 Partner (responsible for client relationship and overall quality)
- 1 Project Leader or Principal (day-to-day engagement management)
- 1-3 Associates/Consultants (primary analysis and output production)
- Client counterparts embedded in the team

## Engagement Model
- Typical engagement: 6-12 weeks
- Work products: slide decks, supporting analytics, implementation artifacts
- Primary output format: PDF/PowerPoint; internal tracking in Confluence
- Primary communication: weekly steering committees with client leadership

## BCG Hierarchy

| Level | Typical Role |
|---|---|
| Partner / Managing Director | Client ownership, final quality sign-off |
| Principal / Associate Director | Day-to-day leadership, review of all outputs |
| Project Leader | Manages work of associates, interface with client PM |
| Consultant | Analysis lead, owns workstream outputs |
| Associate | Analysis execution, model building, slide production |

## Quality Standards

Generic consulting quality standards (so-what-first, MECE discipline,
evidenced claims, sensitivity transparency) live in
`.agent/context/quality-standards.md` and apply to all analytical work,
not just BCG engagements.

BCG-specific gate on top of those:
- **"Ready for client" = Partner has reviewed and approved.** No deliverable
  leaves the team for a client meeting without Partner sign-off. This is
  BCG-specific — generic quality standards are necessary but not sufficient
  for BCG client-readiness.

## Tools
- Confluence: internal knowledge management, case documentation
- Excel/Python/SQL: analytics and modeling
- PowerPoint: client deliverable format
